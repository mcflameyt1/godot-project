Name: Auto Shotgun Zombie
Difficulty: Easy(ish)

Summon: ASGGuy
Melee: No
Distance: Hitscan
Type: Undead
Brightmaps: Yes

A zombie with an automatic shotgun that fires four shots in quick succession at a time and reloads
after sixteen shots. Can take more damage than Doom's standard zombies, and can deal damage to the
player very quickly at close ranges.
